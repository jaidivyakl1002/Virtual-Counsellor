# 🧠 Virtual Counsellor - AI Career Guidance Platform

A modern, AI-powered career guidance platform that combines psychometric science with real-time market intelligence to provide personalized career roadmaps.

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 **Personalized Career Guidance**
- **For School Students**: Navigate high school with confidence using AI-powered aptitude analysis
- **For University Students**: Bridge skill gaps with tailored roadmaps for job readiness
- **For Professionals**: Make strategic career switches with data-driven transition plans

### 🧪 **Advanced Technology Stack**
- **Psychometric Science + Agentic AI**: Validated psychological assessments with specialized AI agents
- **Real-Time Market Intelligence**: Live job market data integration for relevant career advice
- **RAG-Powered Knowledge Base**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation ensures factual, up-to-date information
- **Actionable Roadmaps**: Detailed, milestone-based plans from course selection to skill development


## 🎨 Design System

### **Color Palette**
- **Primary**: `#1E3A8A` (Deep Blue)
- **Secondary**: `#60A5FA` (Light Blue)
- **Accent**: `#3B82F6` (Medium Blue)
- **Background**: `#F8FAFC` (Light Gray)
- **Text**: `#1F2937` (Dark Gray)

### **Typography**
- **Headings**: Space Grotesk (Modern, geometric sans-serif)
- **Body Text**: Inter (Readable, professional sans-serif)
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-bold), 700 (Bold)

### **Components**
- **Cards**: Rounded corners (16px), subtle shadows, hover animations
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds, smooth transitions, glassmorphism effects
- **Icons**: Material-UI icons with consistent sizing and coloring
